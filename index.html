WXY Corporation is a global leader in experience-based reward campaigns, powered by its proprietary SaaS platform COSMOS. The private equity acquisition is underwritten at a maximum valuation of 10.0Ã— LTM EBITDA.

The transaction is structured as a leveraged buyout with approximately 60% debt financing and 40% equity contribution. This model analyzes the investment's viability under various operational and macroeconomic scenarios.

LBO Model Outputs

  • Capital Structure: Debt tranches, equity contribution, and financing fees
  • Cash Flow Waterfall: Debt repayment schedule and cash sweep mechanisms
  • Investment Returns: IRR and MOIC calculations across multiple exit scenarios
  • Credit Metrics: Leverage ratios, interest coverage, and debt capacity analysis
  • Sensitivity Analysis: Impact of revenue growth, margin expansion, and exit multiple assumptions

Model Application

This comprehensive Excel template allows users to:

  • Test underwriting assumptions and investment hypotheses
  • Analyze capital structure optimization opportunities
  • Evaluate downside protection through covenant compliance testing
  • Assess exit strategy alternatives and timing considerations
